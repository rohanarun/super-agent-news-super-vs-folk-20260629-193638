Super vs Folk at a glance

Folk

Folk is best known as a modern, collaborative CRM and contact management tool. It focuses on people, pipelines, and lightweight automations around relationships.

  • Relationship and contact-centric workflows
  • Designed for teams managing deals, partners, or communities
  • Automation lives inside Folk’s own product surface

Super

Super is a personal AI agent that can operate real computers — clicking, typing, navigating apps and websites — and reuse past work through a computer-use cache.

  • Operates full desktops and web apps, not just APIs
  • Ideal for repeated workflows like ops, research, QA, and back-office work
  • Cache reuse lowers repeated execution cost over time

Why Super is sharper for repeated computer-use work

Real computer operation

Unlike tools optimized for data entry or CRM logic, Super agents can directly operate browsers, desktop apps, and internal tools — similar to what we now see emerging across the market.

Computer-use cache

Super reuses prior actions and states through a computer-use cache. When you run the same workflow again, the agent doesn’t start from scratch.

Better economics for repetition

For workflows you repeat daily or weekly, caching makes Super meaningfully more efficient than agents that replay every click every time.
